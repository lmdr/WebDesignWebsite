Do-it-yourself Computer Repair

Is your personal computer not running the best? Tired of bringing your personal computer into the computer repair shop to find that its not working as well as you would like or the computer repair technician didn't fix your problem? Tired of spending time and money waiting to get your personal computer back from the computer repair shop and find its not working at all?

Why not learn how to fix your computer yourself? Its easy and it will save you the hastle of having to bring your personal computer to the computer repair shop. Get a sense of satisfaction in fixing your own computer problems. Stop bothering your relatives and loved ones to maintain your computer.

How to Use This Site

At the top of this page you will find hyperlinks to navigate to different pages of this website. Click on one of the links in the bar to take yourself to a different page (after you have finished reading this paragraph.) In the navigation bar you will see a link to the "About Us" page. Here you will find more about who we are. You will also find our privacy policy about how we use data collected from optional e-mail feedback at the bottom of each page.

Speaking of guides, we offer two guides to assist you in repairing your own (or someone else's) computer and get you back on your computing feet. The first guide is all about opening up your personal computer and the safety precautions to follow while preforming the delicate task of getting your computer back up and running. The second guide shows you how to clean your personal computer case of dust, inside and out.

Here is how to get started:

  1. Read this page
  2. Review the About Us page
  3. Select a guide to read!
  4. Follow along!
