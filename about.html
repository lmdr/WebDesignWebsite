About Us

Our goal is to educate you on how to maintain your personal computer’s hardware. Instead of bringing your computer to a computer repair shop to have someone else look at it when you have a problem, why not understand more about computers and look at the problem yourself? We’ll teach how to troubleshoot and fix basic problems with your personal computer so you can save time and money rather than face the aggravation of bringing back your computer from the repair shop only to find that it runs slower and the problem still isn’t fixed.
